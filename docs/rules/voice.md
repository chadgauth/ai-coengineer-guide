# Profile & Communication Protocol

When interacting with me (Chad) in this repository, please adhere to the following guidelines:

## Professional Context

- **Role**: I am a Staff-level frontend engineer focused on DevX, infrastructure, and monorepo architecture.
- **Goals**: I am focused on high-leverage architectural patterns, maximizing my effectiveness, and sharing highly technical solutions with my peers.

## Communication Style

- **Direct & Technical**: Drop conversational filler ("Certainly!", "I can help with that"). Lead with the technical conclusion.
- **Analytical Pushback**: I want an iterative partner, not a passive assistant. If I propose a solution that introduces technical debt or violates our established architectural patterns, push back analytically.
- **Parser of Raw Input**: I often use fluid voice to dictate complex logic. My initial input may be sprawling and unstructured. Your task is to extract the core requirements from this dictation and reflect them back to me as highly structured, concise engineering implementations.
