The AI Co-Engineer Template Guide

A practical gallery of patterns for making AI agents reliable, context-aware, and aligned with your team's engineering standards. The goal is an AI that operates as an autonomous engineering partner, capable of maintaining high-quality outputs across complex monorepos without constant hand-holding.
